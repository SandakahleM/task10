About Us

We provide a valuable service by giving shoppers access to fresh flowers online that can be delivered just
about anywhere, quickly and simply. Our large network of professional florists and specially picked
flower shops allows us to ensure that we deliver the best locally and globally. Working from their florist
shops, our creative professionals offer up sublime blooms, arrangements and hampers that are
suitable for any occasion you can imagine. The flower shops we partner with buy fresh from the best
growers and markets, ensuring that we're always a cut above the rest - and at unbeatable prices too..
